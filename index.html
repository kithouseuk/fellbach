WHO ARE KITHOUSE?

KitHouse, launched in 2022 between Finland and the United Kingdom, is an online shop dedicated to selling authentic football jerseys from top brands like Adidas, Nike, and Puma. While they don’t manufacture their own jerseys, KitHouse prides itself on offering a curated selection of high-quality kits from popular clubs and national teams around the world. With a growing fan base of over 19,000 followers across all platforms, KitHouse is becoming a trusted destination for football enthusiasts seeking the latest and most iconic jerseys.

Who are SV Fellbach?

SV Fellbach is a passionate and competitive football club based in Fellbach, Germany. Known for their strong community ties and exciting gameplay, the club competes in regional leagues with a focus on developing local talent and engaging fans. Their home games are played at the Stadion an der Mörikestraße, where the team’s vibrant spirit and commitment to the sport shine through in every match.
